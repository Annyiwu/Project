Our purpose

Why gemestone:

Believe it or not, no women could refuse the allure of gemstone and diamond jewelry, and so no men could escape from purchasing such a piece for his wife. However, there are few people who know the successive and complicated stages of processing gemstones from the raw stones. Take a look at this website, and you will understand better about the techniques behind the beauty and rarity of each gemstone. This website will take you into a journey of appreciating gemstone, including basic introduction of gemstones, followed by the sourcing cutting, polishing, application on jewelry and relative environmental impacts during the process.

Let you know:

  • The categories, formation and background of gemstones;
  • The complicated process to make gemstones behind the beauty of them;
  • The application of gemstones in jewelry industry and representative brands;
  • The environmental impacts of manufacturing gemstones and potential sustainable ways as solutions in the future of gemstone industry.
