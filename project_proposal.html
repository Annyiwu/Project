What is my website about?

My website is about taking my audience into a journey of appreciating and understanding gemstones more rather than simply knowing its high value in price. In addition, my website serves to encourage and motivate gemstone lovers and businesses related with gemstone to think about the environmental effects behind the allure of gemstone.

The objective of my website

Since I am interested in gemstone and its application in jewelry, I have looked up for websites to educate me but found few. Thus, the object of my website is to

  • Educate gemstone lovers about the complicated process to make gemstones behind the beauty of them;
  • Emphasize the environmental impacts by inappropriate sourcing;
  • Encourage more sustainable ways of branding and manufacturing gems that businesses could consider

My target audience

  • Jewelry and gemstone lover
  • Businesses related with manufacturing and selling gemstones
